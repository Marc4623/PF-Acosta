Want to work with us?

At Marc's Restorations, we are a well-established restoration facility that has built its reputation on providing our clients with nothing but the best. Over the last 48 years, we've grown and expanded our facility and staff to employ over 30 enthusiastic craftspeople in a creative and positive work environment. As our shop continues to grow, we are looking for motivated, highly-skilled, intelligent technicians, fabricators, upholsterers, and body restoration specialists to join our team. We service and restore classic cars from every era, from vintage racers and weekend warriors to concours-winners and museum pieces. This job involves experience working on classic cars from various manufacturers, including, but not limited to, Mercedes, Jaguar, Ferrari, Porsche, GM, Ford, Chrysler, and countless now-defunct marques. The ideal candidate for this position will be a well-organized, responsible individual who enjoys learning new skills and takes instruction well. Regardless of experience level, this is a specialized and rewarding career opportunity.
