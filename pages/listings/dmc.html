Up for sale is a beautiful 1981 DMC-12 DeLorean in well kept condition and runs extremely well! The sole model produced by the DeLorean Motor Company (DMC), the DeLorean or “DMC-12” was assembled in Northern Ireland and built from January of 1981 up until December of 1982 when the company went bankrupt with approximately 9,000 cars produced. The car is most notable for its distinctive stainless-steel bodywork, exotic gullwing doors, and its role as the time machine in the Back to the Future movie franchise. This 5-speed 1981 example had remained under the care of its second owner for over 25 years, and is now up for sale. With low miles, this delorean at 35,287 miles on the odometer is a show stopper.
