Racing is our passion!

Whether your racing preference is “arrive and drive” or a more hands-on experience, Marc's Racing Services exists to make your time at the track the best it can be. Our experienced automotive technicians will ensure your race car is ready when you are and won’t let you down when the pressure is on. Don’t own a race car? We can guide you to the best purchase or rent one of our top machines. We have a fleet of rental race cars allowing you to test the waters before diving in. We specialize in vintage racecar restoration, preparation & track support. We offer personalized driving instruction, event hospitality services, driver training, and, of course, transportation to and from the track. No detail is left unattended, and you’ll appreciate our one-on-one interactions from start to successful finish. Get the most from your racing experience with us!
